Plan 1

Basic English Language Plan: "Starter"
  • Target Audience: Beginners and those looking to build a strong foundation in English.
  • Description: Course Duration: 3 months. Classes: Two 60-minute live classes per week. Content: Covers basic grammar, essential vocabulary, and everyday conversation skills. Materials: Access to interactive learning materials, exercises, and quizzes. Support: Email support for questions and clarifications. Price: Affordable monthly subscription.

Plan 2

Intermediate English Language Plan: "Progressive"
  • Target Audience: Learners with some prior English knowledge aiming to improve their skills.
  • Description: Course Duration: 6 months. Classes: Three 90-minute live classes per week. Content: In-depth grammar, expanded vocabulary, and practical speaking and writing exercises. Materials: Comprehensive study guides, audiovisual resources, and practice tests. Support: Dedicated instructor support via chat and discussion forums. Extras: Weekly conversation clubs and personalized feedback on assignments. Price: Tiered pricing options with discounts for longer subscriptions.

Plan 3

Advanced English Language Plan: "Fluency Mastery"
  • Target Audience: Advanced learners striving for fluency and advanced proficiency.
  • Description: Course Duration: 12 months. Classes: Four 120-minute live classes per week. Content: Advanced grammar, specialized vocabulary, academic writing, and business communication. Materials: Extensive e-library, access to research articles, and simulated real-world scenarios. Support: 24/7 live chat with instructors, personalized coaching sessions, and priority email assistance. Extras: Cultural immersion experiences, networking events, and access to exclusive resources. Price: Premium pricing with flexible payment options and a satisfaction guarantee.
